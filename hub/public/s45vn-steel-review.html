At a Glance

S45VN is Crucible's official next-generation successor to S35VN, introduced with refined alloy ratios to increase chromium content (improving corrosion resistance) and optimize the carbide size distribution for better batch consistency. In practical blade performance, the improvements over S35VN are incremental — the key differences manifest in long-term edge retention under sustained use and marginally better rust resistance in humid environments. Designed explicitly for the flagship EDC and premium outdoor knife segment, it represents Crucible's current state-of-the-art balanced PM stainless offering. Comparable to S35VN in toughness category, with an edge in corrosion resistance.
