At a Glance

8Cr13MoV is the mid-volume workhorse of Chinese stainless steel production, elevated from the 5Cr15MoV base through a higher carbon content (~0.8%) that enables a meaningful step up in hardness and edge retention. It is chemically near-identical to Japan's AUS-8, and is often positioned as a cost-effective alternative for Western brands sourcing at the mid-tier. It dominates the mid-range private-label kitchen knife and entry EDC segments across North American and European retail.
