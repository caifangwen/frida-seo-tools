At a Glance

N690 is a cobalt-alloyed martensitic stainless steel from Böhler (Austria), built on a high-chromium, high-carbon base with cobalt added to enhance carbide stability and corrosion resistance. It is often described as the European counterpart to VG-10, sharing a similar carbon level but offering superior rust resistance due to higher cobalt content. Frequently specified by European tactical and outdoor knife brands for its combination of edge performance and corrosion durability. Comparable to 440C in chemistry, with better real-world rust resistance in salt-exposure environments.
