At a Glance

S35VN is a powder metallurgy stainless steel from Crucible Industries, developed as a direct upgrade to S30V. The key refinement is the addition of niobium (Nb), which partially replaces vanadium in carbide formation, producing smaller and more evenly distributed carbides. This improves both toughness and sharpenability compared to S30V, while maintaining essentially the same edge retention and corrosion resistance. In the global high-end EDC and outdoor knife market, S35VN has become the de facto baseline PM stainless specification — the steel against which all other premium steels are benchmarked. Comparable to Elmax in corrosion resistance and similar to M390 in category, but with better toughness than both.
