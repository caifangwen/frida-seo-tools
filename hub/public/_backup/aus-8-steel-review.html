At a Glance

AUS-8 is a mid-grade martensitic stainless steel from Aichi Steel (Japan), formulated as an upgrade over AUS-6 with higher carbon content for improved hardness and edge stability. Chemically close to 8Cr13MoV, its competitive advantage lies in tighter mill tolerances and more consistent batch quality — making it the preferred import option for Western brands that need reliable, verifiable steel sourcing without stepping up to premium European or PM steels. It is a direct alternative to 440B in Western classification terms.
