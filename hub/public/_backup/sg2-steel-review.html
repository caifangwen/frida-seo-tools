At a Glance

SG2 is a high-vanadium powder metallurgy stainless steel produced by Takefu Special Steel (Japan), marketed under the name R2 by several knife brands. Its very high vanadium content (~1.45%) combined with PM processing produces exceptionally fine, uniform carbides that enable extreme sharpness at high hardness levels. SG2 is the dominant PM steel in the Japanese premium kitchen knife segment, where its superior edge keenness and good corrosion resistance align precisely with the requirements of professional culinary use. Comparable to Elmax in corrosion performance and similar to ZDP-189 in sharpness potential, but with better toughness than ZDP-189. The functional equivalent of Crucible's S90V in the Japanese production ecosystem.
