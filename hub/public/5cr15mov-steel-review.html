At a Glance

5Cr15MoV is an enhanced martensitic stainless steel developed from the 3Cr13 base, with molybdenum added for corrosion resistance and vanadium for grain refinement and edge stability. Widely regarded as the cost-effective alternative to 440A and 420HC, it delivers comparable real-world performance with better manufacturing consistency for mass production. As one of the top-volume OEM steels globally, it serves as the industry standard for entry-to-mid level kitchen cutlery across Western retail and wholesale channels.
