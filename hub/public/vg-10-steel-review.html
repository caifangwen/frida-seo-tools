At a Glance

VG-10 is a cobalt-enhanced martensitic stainless steel from Takefu Special Steel (Japan), developed specifically for premium kitchen knives. Its cobalt addition refines the carbide structure, improving edge stability at high hardness levels without making the steel brittle at typical kitchen-knife HRC ranges. For three decades, it has been the benchmark mid-high stainless in the Japanese kitchen knife market, and remains one of the most recognized steel names in Western culinary retail. It is widely used as the core layer in Damascus-clad kitchen knives. Functionally comparable to 440C in corrosion resistance, but with better carbide distribution.
