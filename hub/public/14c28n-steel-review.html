At a Glance

14C28N is a nitrogen-enhanced martensitic stainless steel developed by Sandvik (Sweden), engineered specifically for knife blades as an evolution of 13C26 (a traditional razor steel). The nitrogen addition suppresses chromium carbide precipitation, improving both corrosion resistance and toughness without sacrificing hardness potential. It is comparable to 440A in corrosion resistance but outperforms it in edge stability and HT response. Widely adopted by Scandinavian and North American mid-range knife brands for kitchen and outdoor applications.
