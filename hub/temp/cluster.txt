一、Cluster Article 的定位

1.1 在三层体系中的角色

Plain Text
Hub Page = 百科全书的目录页
  → "我们有哪些钢材知识"
  → 导航为主，字数少

Pillar Page = 百科全书的某个章节
  → "厨刀钢材完全指南"
  → 全面覆盖，中等深度

Cluster Article = 某个词条的专著     ← 你现在在这里
  → "VG-10钢材的一切"
  → 只讲一个话题，讲到没有人比你更透彻
  → 单点极致深度

1.2 Cluster 的核心使命

Plain Text
使命1：成为某个具体问题的"终极回答"（Definitive Answer）
  → 用户搜了这个问题之后不需要再看第二篇文章

使命2：成为AI引擎引用的首选信源
  → 定义清晰、数据具体、表格完整、来源可信

使命3：为Hub和Pillar提供深度支撑
  → Hub说"VG-10是热门钢材"，链到这里看详细评测

使命4：在合适的上下文中将读者引向Money Page
  → 不是硬塞广告，是"读完VG-10评测后自然想了解OEM选项"

1.3 Cluster Article 的六种类型

类型	特征	Leeknives示例	Keganico示例
A. 定义/评测型	"What is X" / "X Review"	VG-10 Steel Review	Liner Lock Explained
B. 对比型	"X vs Y"	Santoku vs Chef's Knife	Frame Lock vs Liner Lock
C. 指南/How-To型	"How to do X"	How to Sharpen a Kitchen Knife	How to Clean a Folding Knife
D. 列表/排名型	"Best X for Y" / "Types of X"	Best Wood for Cutting Board	Best Steel for Pocket Knives
E. 法规型	"Laws about X in Y"	（不适用）	Knife Laws in California
F. 问题解答型	回答一个具体问题	Is Bamboo Good for Cutting Boards?	Are Assisted Opening Knives Legal?

每种类型的结构不同，下面逐一给出模板。

URL: site.com/[slug]/

Markdown
[H2] What Is [Subject]?

[定义段落 — AI引用的核心目标]
"[Subject] is [精确定义], [特征1], and [特征2]. 
It is [分类归属/历史背景]. [1-2句补充说明]."

规则：
□ 定义必须在段落第一句
□ 用"[Subject] is..."句型开头
□ 不要用比喻或类比开头，直接给定义
□ 这段话被AI引用的概率最高，必须准确、简洁、完整

例（VG-10）：
"VG-10 (V Gold 10) is a high-carbon stainless steel 
produced by Takefu Special Steel Co. in Japan, containing 
approximately 1% carbon and 15% chromium. It is classified 
as a martensitic stainless steel, widely considered the 
industry standard for mid-to-premium kitchen knives due 
to its balanced combination of edge retention, corrosion 
resistance, and ease of sharpening."

例（Liner Lock）：
"A liner lock is a folding knife locking mechanism in 
which a thin, spring-tempered steel liner inside the 
handle flexes laterally to press against the blade tang, 
holding the blade in the open position. Invented by 
Michael Walker in 1981, it is the most common lock type 
in modern folding knives, used in an estimated 60%+ of 
production folders worldwide."
