## Chapter 1: Types of Non Woven Shopping Bags

### By Handle Type (Most Common Classification)

#### 1. D-Cut Shopping Bags

**Description**: Handles are die-cut directly from the bag body, creating a D-shaped grip.

**Specifications**:
- **GSM Range**: 70-100 GSM
- **Handle Strength**: 10-14 kg capacity
- **Price Range**: $0.25-0.40 per bag (MOQ 1,000 pcs)

**Pros**:
✅ Most economical option  
✅ Simple, clean design  
✅ Good for lightweight shopping  
✅ Fast production turnaround  

**Cons**:
❌ Less comfortable for heavy loads  
❌ Handle can tear under stress  
❌ Limited premium appeal  

**Best For**:
- Supermarkets and grocery stores
- Promotional events
- High-volume, cost-sensitive applications
- Lightweight retail (books, small items)

---

#### 2. U-Cut Shopping Bags

**Description**: Similar to D-cut but with a U-shaped handle profile for slightly better ergonomics.

**Specifications**:
- **GSM Range**: 80-110 GSM
- **Handle Strength**: 10-15 kg capacity
- **Price Range**: $0.28-0.45 per bag

**Pros**:
✅ Slightly more comfortable than D-cut  
✅ Still cost-effective  
✅ Clean aesthetic  
✅ Good weight distribution  

**Cons**:
❌ Similar limitations to D-cut  
❌ Not suitable for very heavy items  

**Best For**:
- General retail shopping
- Department stores
- Pharmacy and cosmetics
- Mid-range applications

---

#### 3. W-Cut Shopping Bags

**Description**: W-shaped die-cut handles provide reinforced grip points.

**Specifications**:
- **GSM Range**: 90-120 GSM
- **Handle Strength**: 12-18 kg capacity
- **Price Range**: $0.32-0.50 per bag

**Pros**:
✅ Better weight distribution  
✅ More comfortable than D/U-cut  
✅ Reinforced handle points  
✅ Medium-duty capability  

**Cons**:
❌ Higher cost than basic die-cut  
❌ Still not premium feel  

**Best For**:
- Clothing stores
- Electronics retailers
- Medium-weight shopping
- Extended carrying comfort

---

#### 4. Soft Loop Handle Bags

**Description**: Separate fabric loops are attached to the bag, creating comfortable shoulder handles.

**Specifications**:
- **GSM Range**: 100-140 GSM
- **Handle Strength**: 18-25 kg capacity
- **Handle Length**: 25-35cm (shoulder carry)
- **Price Range**: $0.40-0.70 per bag

**Pros**:
✅ Most comfortable for heavy loads  
✅ Shoulder carry capability  
✅ Premium appearance  
✅ High durability  
✅ Reusable 100+ times  

**Cons**:
❌ Higher cost than die-cut  
❌ Longer production time  

**Best For**:
- Premium retail stores
- Clothing and fashion boutiques
- Department stores
- Brands emphasizing quality

---

#### 5. Patch Handle Bags

**Description**: Reinforced fabric patch with attached handles provides maximum strength.

**Specifications**:
- **GSM Range**: 120-160 GSM
- **Handle Strength**: 22-30 kg capacity
- **Price Range**: $0.60-1.00 per bag

**Pros**:
✅ Maximum load capacity  
✅ Most durable option  
✅ Luxury appearance  
✅ Extended reusability  
✅ Reinforced stress points  

**Cons**:
❌ Highest cost  
❌ Heavier (more shipping cost)  

**Best For**:
- Luxury brands
- Heavy items (wine, electronics)
- Premium department stores
- Long-term brand investment

## Chapter 9: Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: What's the most popular size for retail shopping bags?

**Medium (30×12×35cm)** is the most versatile and commonly used size, suitable for:
- 10-15 average items
- 2-4 clothing items
- 3-5 books
- Standard grocery trips

Many retailers use a mix: 50% Medium, 30% Large, 20% Small.

### Q2: How many times does the average customer reuse a shopping bag?

**Research findings**:
- Non woven bags: 50-100+ uses average
- Soft loop handle bags: 80-120 uses
- D-cut bags: 30-50 uses
- Cotton bags: 200-500+ uses (but higher environmental cost)

**Key factor**: Bag quality and handle comfort directly impact reuse frequency.

### Q3: Are non woven shopping bags recyclable?

**Yes**, PP non woven bags are recyclable (#5 plastic) where facilities exist. However:
- Recycling infrastructure varies by region
- Bags should be clean and dry before recycling
- Maximum value is achieved through reuse first, then recycling

[→ Learn more: Recyclable Non Woven Bags](./recyclable-non-woven-bags.md)

### Q4: Can I offer different bag sizes to customers?

**Yes**, and recommended for retailers with varied product ranges:
- Small: Add-on purchases, cosmetics, gifts
- Medium: Standard purchases (50% of inventory)
- Large: Bulk purchases, multiple items (30%)
- XL: Exceptional purchases (20%)

**Inventory tip**: Start with 2 sizes, expand based on demand.

### Q5: Should I sell bags or give them free?

**Depends on your strategy**:

**Sell Bags**:
- ✅ Generates revenue
- ✅ Customers value what they pay for
- ✅ Higher reuse rates
- ❌ Some customer resistance

**Give Free**:
- ✅ Customer goodwill
- ✅ Brand visibility guaranteed
- ✅ No purchase friction
- ❌ Ongoing cost

**Hybrid Approach** (Recommended):
- Free with minimum purchase
- Paid for additional bags
- Premium bags for sale
- Loyalty members receive free bags

### Q6: How long does it take to receive custom shopping bags?

**Timeline**:
- Stock bags: 3-7 days
- Custom printed (1,000-5,000 pcs): 15-20 days
- Custom printed (5,000+ pcs): 20-30 days
- Peak season (Q3-Q4): Add 5-10 days

**Plus shipping**:
- Express: 3-7 days
- Air freight: 7-14 days
- Sea freight: 25-40 days

### Q7: What's the minimum order for custom shopping bags?

**MOQ by customization**:
- Stock bags with logo: 500 pcs
- Custom size: 1,000 pcs
- Custom printed: 1,000-2,000 pcs
- Fully custom (OEM): 3,000-5,000 pcs

### Q8: Can non woven bags get wet?

**Unlaminated bags**: Water-resistant for 30+ minutes, but not waterproof. Light rain is fine; prolonged exposure will soak through.

**Laminated bags**: 100% waterproof with PP film coating.

**Recommendation**: For grocery/frozen items, laminated bags are worth the premium.
