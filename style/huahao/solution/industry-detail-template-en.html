Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ
What is the minimum order quantity? Can I order samples?▼

MOQ varies by product: paper bags typically start at 500 pcs, canvas totes at 200 pcs, and velvet pouches at 100 pcs. Sample fees range from $30–$120 depending on complexity and are deductible against bulk orders. Rush orders can also use our sample-free fast track.

How do you guarantee lead times during peak seasons?▼

Standard lead time is 15–20 business days. We lock in peak-season capacity three months in advance. For Black Friday, Christmas, and other major events, we recommend placing orders 6–8 weeks ahead. Express 7-day production is available for urgent orders at an additional fee.

What certifications can you provide? Do you support export?▼

We provide FSC, SGS test reports, ISO 9001, EN13432 compostability, and BSCI social compliance documentation. We regularly export to North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East, and can assist with customs declaration paperwork.

How do you ensure color accuracy?▼

We match Pantone references precisely and provide physical color swatches for approval before production. Every batch undergoes 100% visual inspection. If color deviation exceeds agreed tolerances, we cover the reprint cost.

What payment methods do you accept? Is there a payment term?▼

Standard terms are 50% deposit + 50% balance before shipment. Qualified long-term partners may apply for monthly settlement terms. We accept bank transfer, PayPal, Alibaba Trade Assurance, and Letter of Credit for export orders.
