## Paper Bags by Structure

### 1. SOS Self-Opening Sack (SOS Bag)

**What it is**: A paper bag with a pre-glued gusset that **opens automatically** when the sides are pulled apart.

**Key features**:
- No manual opening required
- Flat-bottom design when opened
- High-speed machine packing compatible
- Available in 1–4 color flexo printing

**Typical sizes**: 120×70×250mm to 400×170×450mm

**Best for**:
- 🍔 Fast food restaurants
- 🥖 Bakeries & bread shops
- 🍿 Snack food packaging

**GSM range**: 60–90 GSM kraft paper

> 📖 Related: Learn why [fast food chains prefer SOS bags vs pinch bottom bags](09-restaurant-takeout-paper-bags.md)

---

### 2. Pinch Bottom Bag

**What it is**: A paper bag with a **folded and glued bottom gusset** that expands when filled.

**Key features**:
- Expandable capacity (2–4× flat volume)
- Strong bottom seal
- Can be machine-filled or hand-packed

**Best for**:
- 🛒 Grocery items
- 🌾 Flour, sugar, grains
- 🐾 Pet food packaging

**GSM range**: 70–120 GSM

---

### 3. Square Bottom Bag

**What it is**: A paper bag with a **flat, rigid bottom panel** that allows the bag to stand upright on shelves.

**Key features**:
- Self-standing display
- Premium retail appearance
- Can include window patching

**Best for**:
- 👗 Clothing retail
- 🎁 Gift packaging
- 📦 E-commerce shipping

**GSM range**: 100–180 GSM

> 📖 Related: See how [clothing stores choose the right paper bags](07-paper-bags-for-clothing-stores.md)

## Paper Bags by Handle Type

### 4. Twisted Handle Kraft Paper Bag

**What it is**: A paper bag with a **twisted paper rope handle**, the most iconic and recognizable retail bag style.

**Key features**:
- Handle material: twisted kraft paper cord (3–6mm diameter)
- Weight capacity: 5–15 kg (depending on GSM and construction)
- Available with or without lamination
- Inner patch reinforces handle attachment

**Typical sizes**: 210×110×260mm to 450×150×380mm

**Best for**:
- 👔 Fashion & apparel
- 👜 Luxury brands
- 🛍️ Department stores

**GSM range**: 80–150 GSM kraft paper

**Handle color options**: Natural kraft, white kraft, black kraft, custom colors

> 📖 Related: Compare [twisted handle vs flat handle bags](#) in our material guide

---

### 5. Flat Handle Paper Bag

**What it is**: A paper bag with a **flat paper ribbon handle**, offering a sleeker, more minimalist aesthetic.

**Key features**:
- Handle material: flat paper strip (15–30mm wide)
- Lower profile than twisted handle
- Ideal for lightweight items (2–5 kg)

**Best for**:
- 💄 Cosmetics & beauty
- 💍 Jewelry boutiques
- 📚 Bookstores

**GSM range**: 100–150 GSM

---

### 6. Die-Cut Handle Bag

**What it is**: A paper bag with a **handle cut directly into the bag body**, eliminating the need for separate handle material.

**Key features**:
- No additional handle attachment cost
- Modern, clean design
- Limited weight capacity (1–3 kg)

**Best for**:
- 🎪 Trade shows & events
- 🎉 Promotional campaigns
- 🍩 Donut & pastry shops

**GSM range**: 120–200 GSM (thicker for handle strength)

---

### 7. Patch Handle Bag

**What it is**: A paper bag with a **reinforced handle** featuring an internal or external patch for extra strength.

**Key features**:
- Highest load-bearing capacity among handle bags
- Patch can be inside (hidden) or outside (visible design element)
- Ideal for heavy items (8–20 kg)

**Best for**:
- 🍷 Wine & spirits
- 📦 Heavy retail goods
- 🎁 Premium gift packaging

**GSM range**: 120–200 GSM

---

## Paper Bags by Application

### 8. Flat Paper Bag (No Handle / Sack Bag)

**What it is**: A simple paper bag **without handles**, typically used for small items or food products.

**Key features**:
- Most economical option
- Can be open-mouth or valve-type
- Fast packing speed

**Best for**:
- 🥖 Bread & baguettes
- 🍪 Cookies & biscuits
- 🌰 Nuts & dry foods

**GSM range**: 40–80 GSM

---

### 9. Laminated Paper Bag

**What it is**: A paper bag with a **BOPP or PE lamination film** applied to the surface for water and grease resistance.

**Key features**:
- Moisture-resistant surface
- Enhanced print vibrancy (gloss/matte finish)
- Trade-off: less recyclable than non-laminated bags

**Best for**:
- 🧴 Products requiring moisture barrier
- 🌧️ Outdoor retail use
- 🍔 Greasy food items

> 📖 Related: Read about [laminated vs non-laminated paper bags](06-paper-bag-materials.md)

---

### 10. Multi-Wall Paper Bag

**What it is**: A heavy-duty paper bag constructed with **2–4 layers of kraft paper**, designed for industrial and bulk applications.

**Key features**:
- Extreme durability (25–50 kg capacity)
- Valve or open-mouth options
- Can include PE inner liner

**Best for**:
- 🏭 Industrial raw materials
- 🌾 Agricultural products
- 📦 Wholesale bulk goods

**GSM range**: 70–120 GSM per layer (total 140–480 GSM)

## How to Choose the Right Paper Bag Type

Use this decision framework:

```
Step 1: What are you packaging?
  ├─ Food items → SOS bag, Pinch bottom, Flat bag
  ├─ Retail products → Twisted handle, Square bottom
  ├─ Heavy items (>5kg) → Patch handle, Multi-wall
  └─ Promotional → Die-cut handle

Step 2: What weight capacity do you need?
  ├─ Light (1–3 kg) → Die-cut, Flat handle
  ├─ Medium (3–8 kg) → Twisted handle, Flat handle
  └─ Heavy (8–20 kg) → Patch handle, Reinforced twisted

Step 3: Do you need moisture resistance?
  ├─ Yes → Laminated paper bag
  └─ No → Standard kraft paper (more recyclable)

Step 4: What's your budget?
  ├─ Lowest → Flat bag (no handle)
  ├─ Mid-range → Twisted handle, Die-cut
  └─ Premium → Patch handle, Laminated luxury
```

## FAQ: Types of Paper Bags

### Q1: What is the difference between SOS bag and pinch bottom bag?
SOS bags open automatically and are ideal for high-speed packing machines. Pinch bottom bags have a manually folded gusset and better suit hand-packing or medium-speed operations.

### Q2: Which paper bag type has the highest load capacity?
**Patch handle bags** with reinforced construction can hold 15–20 kg, making them the strongest option for retail packaging.

### Q3: Are laminated paper bags recyclable?
Laminated bags with BOPP film are **more difficult to recycle** than non-laminated bags. If recyclability is a priority, choose non-laminated bags with water-based varnish instead.

### Q4: Can I mix different sizes in one order?
Most manufacturers allow **2–3 sizes per order** with a combined MOQ of 10,000–30,000 pcs. Confirm with your supplier.

### Q5: What GSM should I choose for clothing store bags?
For clothing retail, we recommend **100–130 GSM kraft paper** with twisted handles. This provides adequate strength for 3–8 kg loads while maintaining a premium feel.

### Q6: How do I request a sample?
Contact your **paper bag manufacturer** with your dimensions, material preference, and design. Expect to receive a PPS (Pre-Production Sample) within 5–10 days.
