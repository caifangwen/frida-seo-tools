FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions we receive from premium skincare brands evaluating packaging partners. If your question is not covered here, our atelier team is available for direct consultation.

How do you protect light-sensitive ingredients like retinol and vitamin C?

We offer complete light-blocking solutions — black foil-lined pouches, UV-filtering window films, and opaque rigid boxes. All materials are tested to block 99.9% of UV and visible light. For maximum protection, we recommend pairing an outer rigid box with an inner foil-lined pouch, creating dual-layer defense against photo-degradation during both storage and travel.

Can packaging accommodate airless pump bottles and droppers?

Yes. Our CNC-cut inserts are designed to exact bottle dimensions including pump height and dropper clearance. We test with your actual bottles to ensure secure transit fit. For airless pump systems, we engineer specific cavity depths that protect the pump mechanism while preventing pressure activation during shipping.

What is the minimum order for premium skincare packaging?

Standard MOQ is 500 sets. For limited editions or product launches, we offer 200-set minimums with adjusted pricing. Our digital print capabilities make small-batch luxury viable for indie brands and seasonal collections.

How do refill programs work with custom packaging?

We design permanent outer boxes with standardized inner dimensions. Refill cartridges slide into the same outer packaging, creating the "new product" feel with reduced environmental impact. Our refill systems include progress windows, satisfying click mechanisms, and compostable cartridge sleeves.

Can you integrate regulatory claims into packaging design?

Absolutely. We design claim badges, certification logos, and ingredient callouts that are prominent, credible, and aligned with your brand aesthetic. We also verify compliance with cosmetic labeling regulations across US, EU, and Asian markets.

Is sustainable packaging available without compromising luxury feel?

Yes. Our sustainable luxury line includes mushroom-based foam, algae inks, recycled velvet, and FSC-certified boards that often feel more premium than conventional alternatives. Sustainability has become a luxury signal — our materials prove it.
