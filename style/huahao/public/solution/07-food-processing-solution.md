## ❓ FAQ

**Q1: How do one-way valve coffee bags work?**  
A: The valve allows CO2 (released by fresh coffee) to escape without letting oxygen in, preventing oxidation while maintaining freshness.

**Q2: What barrier level do I need for my product?**  
A: It depends on your product's sensitivity to oxygen and moisture. We conduct free testing to recommend the optimal structure.

**Q3: Can your bags be used for liquid products?**  
A: Yes, we produce liquid-capable pouches with strong seals and appropriate material structures for liquids up to 5L.

**Q4: How does zipper quality affect shelf life?**  
A: A quality zipper maintains barrier properties after opening. We use food-grade zippers that seal tightly to extend post-opening freshness.

**Q5: Do you support small-batch custom printing?**  
A: Yes, we offer digital printing for orders as low as 500 pieces, ideal for test marketing and limited editions.
