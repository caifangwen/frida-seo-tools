**Q1: Do you offer discounts for educational institutions?**  
A: Yes, we provide tiered pricing for bulk orders and special discounts for registered educational institutions.

**Q2: How durable are the canvas bags for student use?**  
A: Our 12oz-16oz canvas bags are designed for daily use, with reinforced stress points and colorfast printing that withstands washing.
