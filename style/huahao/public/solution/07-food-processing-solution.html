Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our food processing freshness packaging solutions. Our experienced technical team understands food preservation science and distribution requirements, and is available to provide specific guidance on barrier optimization, closure systems, and shelf life extension for your food products.

How do one-way valve coffee bags work?

The degassing valve allows CO2 (naturally released by freshly roasted coffee beans) to escape from the package without letting oxygen enter. This prevents oxidation and staleness while maintaining the fresh-roasted flavor and aroma. The one-way mechanism ensures your coffee stays fresh for months while preventing bag bloating during transport and storage.

What barrier level do I need for my product?

The optimal barrier level depends entirely on your product's specific sensitivity to oxygen and moisture exposure. We conduct comprehensive free testing using your actual products to measure degradation rates under various barrier configurations. Our technical team then recommends the optimal material structure that balances shelf life extension with packaging cost-effectiveness for your specific application.

Can your bags be used for liquid products?

Yes, we produce specialized liquid-capable stand-up pouches with reinforced strong seals and appropriate multi-layer material structures designed specifically for liquid products. Our liquid pouches safely contain volumes up to 5 liters with leak-proof performance and are suitable for sauces, oils, beverages, and other liquid food products requiring barrier protection.

How does zipper quality affect shelf life?

A quality zipper closure maintains the package's barrier properties even after initial opening, which is critical for post-opening freshness maintenance. We use premium food-grade zippers that seal tightly and consistently, preventing oxygen and moisture ingress after each use. This extends post-opening freshness significantly and reduces product waste for consumers while maintaining quality throughout the usage period.

Our digital printing maintains excellent color reproduction and brand quality while providing the flexibility to order smaller quantities without compromising packaging professionalism or retail presentation.
