- **Shipping damage = brand damage**: A crushed box or torn bag kills customer perception before they see the product

## 🏷️ Material Selection Guide

| Material | Protection | Weight | Cost | Sustainability | Best For |
|----------|-----------|--------|------|---------------|----------|
| Poly Mailer (co-ex) | Good (tear/puncture) | Ultra-light | $ | Recyclable at store drop-off | Soft goods, non-fragile |
| Kraft Mailer (150gsm) | Medium | Light | $$ | Curbside recyclable | Eco-brands, paper-friendly |
| Bubble Mailer | Excellent (shock) | Medium | $$$ | Store drop-off (mixed) | Fragile, cosmetics, glass |
| Rigid Mailer | Excellent (bend-proof) | Medium | $$ | Curbside recyclable | Books, documents, electronics |
| Compostable Mailer | Good | Light | $$$ | Home compostable | Sustainability-first brands |

**Decision Tip**: For fashion/apparel, poly mailers reduce shipping weight by 60% vs. paper. For eco-conscious positioning, our lightweight kraft mailers with reinforced seams offer the best paper alternative. Bubble mailers should be reserved for genuinely fragile items — they cost 3x and complicate recycling.

## ❓ FAQ

**Q1: Poly mailer or kraft mailer — which is better for my brand?**
A: Poly mailers cost less, weigh less, and resist weather better — ideal for soft goods and cost-sensitive shipping. Kraft mailers are curbside recyclable and align with eco-positioning — ideal for brands where sustainability is a core value proposition.

**Q2: How do I protect fragile items without bubble mailers?**
A: We recommend rigid mailers for flat fragile items (books, electronics) and kraft mailers with molded fiber or paper-based cushioning for irregular shapes. These are recyclable and often lighter than bubble alternatives.

**Q3: Can I print different designs within one order?**
A: Yes. Digital print supports multiple designs in one production run with no plate costs. This is ideal for seasonal variations, limited editions, or A/B testing unboxing designs.

**Q4: How do "right-size" mailers reduce shipping costs?**
A: Carriers charge by dimensional weight (length × width × height ÷ divisor) when it exceeds actual weight. Oversized packaging inflates this calculation. Our right-size program typically reduces shipping costs 15-35%.

**Q5: What about packaging for high-return categories like fashion?**
A: We offer dual-adhesive mailers that customers can re-seal for returns. This eliminates the need for separate return packaging and improves the returns experience. Alternatively, include a prepaid return mailer in the original shipment.

**Q6: Can mailers be personalized per customer?**
A: Digital print enables variable data printing — customer names, order-specific messages, or region-specific content — at a minimal per-unit cost for orders of 1,000+.
