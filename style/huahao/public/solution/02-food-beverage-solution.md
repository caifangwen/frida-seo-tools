## 🎯 Industry Pain Points

- **Food safety compliance**: Strict regulations for food-contact materials (FDA, GB standards)
- **Oil and water resistance**: Takeout and delivery packaging must prevent leakage
- **Temperature tolerance**: Hot and cold food applications require different material properties
- **Brand visibility**: Packaging serves as mobile advertising in delivery scenarios
- **Cost efficiency**: High-volume daily operations need economical yet quality solutions

## ❓ FAQ

**Q1: Are your food packaging bags FDA approved?**  
A: Yes, all our food-contact materials comply with FDA 21 CFR regulations. We provide test reports with each order.

**Q2: Can the bags handle hot foods?**  
A: Our standard bags withstand up to 80°C. For higher temperatures (up to 120°C), we offer special heat-resistant materials.

**Q3: How effective is the oil resistance?**  
A: Our PE-coated bags achieve Kit level 6-8 resistance, suitable for most oily foods. Custom coatings available for extreme cases.

**Q4: Do you provide tamper-evident options?**  
A: Yes, we offer tamper-evident seals, security stickers, and tear-off strips for delivery safety.
