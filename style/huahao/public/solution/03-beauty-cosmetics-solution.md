## 🎯 Industry Pain Points

- **Premium perception requirement**: Packaging must reflect high-end brand positioning
- **Limited edition frequency**: Frequent product launches and collaborations need rapid turnaround
- **Gift presentation**: Unboxing experience directly impacts brand perception and social sharing
- **Material compatibility**: Packaging must not interact with cosmetic products
- **Sustainability expectations**: Beauty consumers increasingly demand eco-conscious packaging
