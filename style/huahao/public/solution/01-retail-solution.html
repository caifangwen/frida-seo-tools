Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our retail packaging solutions. For additional information or specific inquiries, our dedicated support team is always ready to assist you.

What is the minimum order quantity for custom retail bags?

Standard MOQ is 500 pieces for regular customization. For premium options requiring specialized materials, hot foil stamping, or unique finishes, we offer flexible 100-piece small batch services to accommodate boutique and luxury brand requirements.

Can you match our exact brand colors?

Absolutely. We support precise Pantone color matching to ensure brand consistency across all packaging elements. Pre-production samples are provided for color approval, and our advanced color management system guarantees accurate reproduction of your brand palette.

How eco-friendly are your retail bags?

We offer comprehensive eco-friendly options including FSC-certified papers, 100% recycled kraft papers, and biodegradable lamination alternatives. All materials meet or exceed EU environmental standards, and we provide full sustainability documentation for your reporting needs.

What file formats do you accept for artwork?

We accept industry-standard file formats including AI (Adobe Illustrator), PDF, PSD (Photoshop), and high-resolution JPG/PNG files (minimum 300 DPI). Our experienced design team also provides complimentary design assistance and artwork optimization services.

Can you handle rush orders for seasonal campaigns?

Yes, we maintain an express production channel specifically for time-sensitive projects. Rush orders can be delivered within 7-10 days from artwork approval, accommodating urgent seasonal campaigns and promotional launches. Additional expedited fees apply for priority processing.
