Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our educational packaging solutions. Our experienced team understands institutional procurement processes and is available to provide specific guidance on budget optimization, printing methods, and multi-school order coordination for your educational institution.

Do you offer discounts for educational institutions?

Yes, we provide comprehensive tiered pricing structures for bulk orders and special discounts specifically for registered educational institutions. Our volume discount programs become more advantageous as order quantities increase, and we offer additional savings for multi-school consolidation orders and district-wide purchasing agreements.

How durable are the canvas bags for student use?

Our 12oz-16oz canvas bags are specifically engineered for daily student use, featuring reinforced stress points at handle attachments and bottom seams. The colorfast printing withstands repeated washing without fading, and the heavy-duty canvas construction handles textbooks, laptops, and sports equipment while maintaining structural integrity throughout the academic year.

Can you handle multiple school logos in one order?

Absolutely. We specialize in consolidated orders with individual customization for each participating institution, making us ideal for school districts, university systems, or multi-campus organizations. This approach maximizes volume discounts while ensuring each school maintains its unique brand identity on the same base product.

What printing method is best for school logos?

Silk screen printing is the most cost-effective option for simple school logos with limited colors, providing excellent durability for frequent use. For complex designs with gradients or photographic elements, we recommend heat transfer or digital printing methods which capture finer detail. Our design team can advise on the optimal method based on your specific logo requirements.

Are your materials safe for children?

Absolutely. All our educational materials pass rigorous EN71 toy safety and SGS heavy metal testing protocols. We use exclusively non-toxic, water-based inks and child-safe components throughout production. Our OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certification ensures textile materials contain no harmful substances, providing complete safety for students of all ages from elementary through university level.
