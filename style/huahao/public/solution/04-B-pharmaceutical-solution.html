Challenges

Industry Pain Points

Pharmaceutical packaging sits at the intersection of patient safety, regulatory compliance, and supply chain security. Every design decision carries consequences for drug efficacy, brand reputation, and legal liability. These are the critical challenges pharmaceutical manufacturers face today.

Regulatory Non-Negotiables

FDA, EMA, ICH, and national pharmacopoeias leave zero room for interpretation on packaging.

Child Resistance vs Senior Access

The paradox of keeping children out while ensuring elderly patients can open their medication.

Counterfeit Proliferation

Fake medications kill hundreds of thousands annually; packaging must be the first line of defense.

Stability Protection

APIs degrade with moisture, oxygen, and light; packaging must extend shelf life and efficacy.

Serialization Mandates

US DSCSA, EU FMD, and global track-and-trace laws require unique identifiers on every package.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most moisture-resistant pharmaceutical packaging?

Aclar (PCTFE) film provides the highest moisture barrier (<0.02 g/m²/day WVTR), followed by Alu-Alu cold form. For extreme moisture sensitivity, we recommend Aclar laminates or aluminum foil strip packs.

How do you balance child resistance with senior access?

We test all child-resistant designs against both FTM 1 (child) and ISO 13127 (senior) protocols. Senior-friendly features include larger grips, intuitive opening directions, and reduced force requirements while maintaining child resistance.

Can you handle global serialization requirements?

Yes. We provide GS1 DataMatrix, QR codes, and RFID integration compliant with US DSCSA, EU FMD, Russia Chestny ZNAK, and other national track-and-trace systems.

What is the difference between hard and soft tempered aluminum foil?

Hard-tempered foil (H18) is stiff and used for push-through blister lidding. Soft-tempered foil (O) is pliable and used for strip packs and cold-form applications where conformity to cavity shape is needed.

Do you provide stability testing with packaging?

We partner with certified stability laboratories to conduct ICH Q1A accelerated and real-time stability studies with our packaging, providing data packages for regulatory submissions.

How do you prevent pharmaceutical counterfeiting?

We offer layered authentication: overt (holograms, color-shifting ink), covert (UV/IR features, micro-text), and forensic (DNA taggants, chemical markers). Each layer adds protection without cost-prohibitive complexity.
