## 🎯 Industry Pain Points

- **Moisture betrayal**: Crispy croissants go soggy, delicate macarons absorb ambient humidity — packaging must actively manage moisture
- **Grease strike-through**: Butter-heavy pastries stain through standard paper in minutes, creating mess and brand embarrassment
- **Visual appetite**: Customers eat with their eyes first; packaging must showcase the product while protecting it
- **Structural fragility**: Layer cakes, cream puffs, and decorated cookies need packaging that prevents crushing and smearing
- **Time-sensitive freshness**: Bakery items have hours, not days, of peak quality — packaging must extend that window

## 🏷️ Material Selection Guide

| Material | Grease Resistance | Moisture Control | Visibility | Best For |
|----------|------------------|------------------|------------|----------|
| PE-Coated Kraft | Excellent (Kit 6-8) | Good | None | Fried items, buttery pastries |
| Silicone-Coated Kraft | Excellent | Good | None | Eco-focused brands, dry goods |
| Window Bag (PLA film) | Good | Fair | Excellent | Decorated items, sandwiches |
| Rigid Board + Window | N/A (structural) | Vents available | Excellent | Cakes, tarts, fragile items |
| Perforated Kraft | Fair | Excellent (breathable) | None | Artisan bread, crust retention |

**Decision Tip**: For items with high butter/oil content (croissants, puff pastry), PE-coated kraft is essential. For items where appearance sells (decorated cupcakes, macarons), window bags or rigid boxes with windows drive purchase intent.

- **Solution**: Double PE-coated kraft with embossed texture, grease-lock bottom, and "Buttery Goodness" branding embracing the indulgence

- **Challenge**: Delicate macarons arrived at parties cracked, stale, or with colors bled — damaging premium gift positioning
- **Solution**: Individual cell pockets in rigid tray, food-grade silica gel sachet, clear lid with gold foil logo sleeve, and "Handle with Sweetness" messaging

## ❓ FAQ

**Q1: Will grease-proof bags really hold back butter and oil?**
A: Yes. Our PE-coated bags achieve Kit level 6-8 grease resistance, tested with 20% butterfat content items held for 2 hours. For extreme cases (fried donuts, churros), we offer double-coated options.

**Q2: Can window bags keep pastries fresh?**
A: Window bags with PLA film provide moderate moisture barrier. For items needing longer freshness (4-6 hours), we recommend solid grease-proof bags or rigid boxes with ventilation holes.

**Q3: What is the best packaging for decorated cakes?**
A: Rigid cake boxes with clear windows, reinforced corners, and custom inserts prevent smearing and crushing. Available with handles for safe transport and in sizes from 6" to multi-tier.

**Q4: Are your inks safe for direct food contact?**
A: All bakery packaging uses FDA-compliant, water-based food-safe inks. For items with potential direct contact (unwrapped bread), we use barrier layers or food-grade tissue.

**Q5: Can bread bags prevent mold?**
A: Our vented bread bags allow moisture escape while preventing crust softening. For extended shelf life, we offer micro-perforated options that balance breathability with contamination protection.

**Q6: How quickly can seasonal designs be produced?**
A: Digital print enables seasonal campaign bags in 5-7 days. We recommend developing a template system (consistent structure, interchangeable print) for rapid seasonal turnarounds.
