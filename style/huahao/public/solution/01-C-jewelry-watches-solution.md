## 🎯 Industry Pain Points

- **Microscopic scale, massive stakes**: A 2cm ring box demands the same brand precision as a garment bag — but with zero tolerance for fit errors
- **High-value security**: Packaging must deter theft during transit and in-store while maintaining elegance
- **Multi-layer presentation**: Outer bag, inner box, cushion, certificate, care card — every layer must coordinate
- **Material sensitivity**: Metals, gemstones, and leather straps react to certain materials; packaging must be chemically inert
- **Gift urgency**: 40%+ of jewelry purchases are gifts with hard deadlines (Valentine's, anniversaries, holidays)

## ❓ FAQ

**Q1: Can you create packaging that fits my exact jewelry box dimensions?**
A: Yes. We accept physical samples or CAD files and guarantee ±1mm fit tolerance. A physical prototype is always produced for approval before bulk production.

**Q2: Will your velvet or lining materials tarnish silver or gold?**
A: No. All our jewelry-grade materials are SGS-tested and certified free of sulfur, chloride, and other tarnish-inducing compounds. Certificates available on request.

**Q3: What security options do you offer for high-value pieces?**
A: We offer tamper-evident seals, holographic stickers, serial numbering, and RFID/NFC integration. For transit, we provide reinforced security bags with tear indicators.

**Q4: Can packaging be personalized per customer (names, dates, initials)?**
A: Yes. We offer hot foil personalization with 2-3 day turnaround for bridal and gift orders. Minimum 50 pieces for personalized runs.

**Q5: Do you provide packaging for watch brands with specific crown/strap protection needs?**
A: Absolutely. We design custom EVA foam inserts with cutouts for crowns, buckles, and straps. Protective film options available for polished surfaces.

**Q6: What is the smallest order quantity for custom jewelry packaging?**
A: Standard MOQ is 500 sets (outer bag + inner pouch). For bridal or custom editions, we offer 200-set minimums at adjusted pricing.
