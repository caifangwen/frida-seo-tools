Material Science

Material Selection Guide

Choosing the right material is a trade-off between thermal performance, moisture control, unit cost, and end-of-life sustainability. The matrix below helps you navigate those decisions based on your menu mix and brand priorities.

Material Temp Retention Condensation Control Cost Sustainability Best For
Kraft + Foil Lining 15-20 min hot Good $$ Recyclable (separate) Standard hot drinks
Metallized Film + Foam 30-45 min cold Excellent $$$ Store drop-off Cold brew, bubble tea
Molded Pulp Insert N/A (structural) Absorbent $ Compostable Multi-cup carriers
Double-Wall Kraft 10-15 min hot Fair $ Curbside recyclable Budget hot drinks
PLA Lined Kraft 15-20 min hot Good $$$ Compostable Eco-focused brands
Table 2: Material Comparison for Coffee & Tea Packaging

Decision Tip: For hot drinks within 15-minute consumption, double-wall kraft sleeves offer the best cost-to-function ratio. For delivery or cold drinks, metallized foam bags are essential — condensation-soaked paper bags are the number one customer complaint we see in the field.

Common Questions

FAQ

How long will hot drinks stay hot in your bags?

Standard insulated bags maintain temperatures above 60°C for 15-20 minutes. Premium options with thicker insulation extend to 30-45 minutes — ideal for delivery.

Will cold drink bags prevent condensation from soaking through?

Yes. Our cold-drink bags feature a moisture barrier and absorbent layer that traps condensation internally. The exterior remains dry even on humid days.

Can bags be customized to fit our specific cup sizes?

Absolutely. We engineer bags to your exact cup dimensions, ensuring snug fit without tipping. Physical fit tests are conducted before production approval.

What is the most sustainable option for coffee bags?

For hot drinks, double-wall kraft with water-based inks is fully curbside recyclable. For cold drinks, our PLA-lined compostable bags offer the best eco-option, though they require industrial composting facilities.

Do multi-cup carriers really prevent spills?

Our molded pulp inserts hold each cup securely with a 2cm lip, preventing tipping even when the bag is jostled. Tested to simulate rush-hour carrying conditions.

Can I do seasonal designs without high plate costs?

Yes. Digital printing supports seasonal campaigns with no plate setup, allowing 4-6 design changes per year cost-effectively for orders of 1,000+.
