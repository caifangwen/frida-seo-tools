Challenges

Industry Pain Points

Supplements and nutraceuticals operate in one of the most competitive and regulated consumer categories. Packaging must simultaneously protect sensitive active ingredients, communicate trust and efficacy, and navigate a complex web of channel-specific requirements — all while standing out on crowded shelves and screens.

Oxidation and Degradation

Omega-3s, probiotics, and vitamins lose potency with oxygen and moisture exposure — packaging must be the active barrier.

Regulatory Gray Zones

Supplements sit between food and drugs; labeling and claims must navigate FDA, EFSA, and TGA without crossing into drug territory.

Amazon/Retail Dual Requirements

E-commerce needs shipping durability; retail needs shelf presence — often with different packaging.

Dosing Compliance

Patients forget doses; packaging that aids adherence improves efficacy perception and repurchase.

Skepticism Barrier

A crowded market of dubious claims means packaging must signal legitimacy and quality instantly.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What packaging do I need for probiotics to maintain viability?

Probiotics require complete oxygen and moisture barriers. We recommend aluminum foil pouches with oxygen absorber and desiccant, nitrogen-flushed during filling, with oxygen indicator if possible.

How do I prevent fish oil oxidation and fishy aftertaste?

Oxygen barrier packaging is essential — glass bottles with nitrogen flush and tight-seal caps, or high-barrier foil pouches. We also recommend antioxidant formulation (vitamin E) and enteric coating.

Can supplement packaging be both child-resistant and senior-friendly?

Yes. We offer senior-friendly CRC designs with larger grips, intuitive opening, and reduced force requirements while maintaining 16 CFR 1700 compliance.

What is the best packaging for Amazon FBA supplements?

Puncture-resistant multi-layer bags or rigid bottles with leak-proof seals. We provide ISTA 6-Amazon testing to ensure your packaging survives Amazon's fulfillment network.

How do you support personalized supplement packaging?

Digital print enables variable data — customer names, daily dosing schedules, and personalized nutrient combinations — at volume scale with no plate costs.

Is sustainable supplement packaging possible without compromising shelf life?

Yes. Our compostable barrier films and post-consumer recycled bottles maintain the same barrier performance as conventional materials, validated through accelerated stability testing.
