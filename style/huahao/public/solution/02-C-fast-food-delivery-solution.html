Market Challenges

Industry Pain Points

Fast food and delivery packaging operates under extreme constraints: sub-30-second packing speed, aggressive handling by couriers, temperature decay over delivery distance, and the constant pressure to keep per-unit cost in the cents. When packaging fails, the damage shows up instantly as refunds, one-star reviews, and eroded brand trust.

Speed Is Everything

Orders must be packaged in under 30 seconds during peak; complex packaging slows throughput and kills margins.

Leakage Nightmares

Sauces, soups, and drinks escape during transport, creating negative reviews and refund requests.

Temperature Collapse

Hot food arrives lukewarm; cold drinks arrive room-temperature — delivery destroys the kitchen experience.

Cost at Scale

Chains serve millions of orders monthly; packaging costs must stay in the cents, not dollars.

Brand Anonymity

Third-party delivery platforms hide the brand; packaging is the only marketing touchpoint left.

Common Questions

FAQ

Will your bags really prevent leaks during delivery?

Our double-coated grease-proof bags with sealed gusset bottoms are tested with 200ml of liquid sauce for 30 minutes of simulated delivery. For extreme liquid items (soups), we recommend cup-in-bag configurations or rigid containers.

How hot will food stay during 20-30 minute delivery?

Standard foil-insulated bags maintain food above 60°C for 20-25 minutes in ambient conditions. For longer delivery windows or extreme climates, our non-woven insulated bags extend this to 40-45 minutes.

Do tamper-evident seals really work?

Yes. Our adhesive seals use break-pattern technology — once opened, the damage is clearly visible and cannot be resealed without detection. We also offer numbered seals for order-audit trails.

What is the most cost-effective solution for a 500-location chain?

For in-store service, 120gsm PE-coated kraft with 1-2 color flexo print offers the best cost-performance ratio at scale. For delivery-heavy operations, the upgrade to foil-insulated bags pays for itself in reduced complaints and refunds.

Can bags be designed for sub-30-second packaging speed?

Yes. We design with operational speed in mind — easy-open stacks, pre-formed gussets, and intuitive loading geometry. We conduct time-motion studies with your crew to optimize workflow.

How do I make my brand visible on third-party delivery apps?

Packaging is your secret weapon. Bold, distinctive bag designs photograph well and create "brand recognition moments" when drivers carry them through neighborhoods. We also integrate QR codes linking directly to your ordering platform, bypassing aggregator fees.
