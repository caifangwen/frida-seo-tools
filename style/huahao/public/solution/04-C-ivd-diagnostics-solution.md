## 🎯 Industry Pain Points

- **Biohazard containment**: Diagnostic specimens and reagents carry infectious agents; packaging must prevent leakage and exposure
- **Result accuracy dependency**: A compromised reagent or specimen invalidates the test — packaging errors become diagnostic errors
- **Temperature fragility**: Reagents, control materials, and specimens often require strict 2-8°C or frozen conditions
- **Multi-modal shipping**: IVD products travel by air, ground, and courier with varying handling quality — packaging must survive all
- **Regulatory diversity**: CE-IVD, FDA 510(k), WHO prequalification — each market has distinct packaging and labeling demands

## ❓ FAQ

**Q1: What is a 95kPa biohazard pouch and when is it required?**
A: A 95kPa pouch withstands 95 kilopascals of pressure differential without leaking. It's required by IATA for transporting UN 3373 Category B biological substances (human and animal specimens) by air.

**Q2: How long can your cold chain shippers maintain temperature?**
A: Standard configurations maintain 2-8°C for 48-72 hours and -20°C for 24-48 hours in summer ambient conditions. We validate each configuration with actual shipping lane data and ISTA 7E testing.

**Q3: Can you package at-home diagnostic tests for consumers rather than labs?**
A: Yes. We specialize in "clinical-grade, consumer-friendly" packaging — rigid boxes, soft-touch finishes, illustrated instructions, and discreet outer shipping — while maintaining all regulatory requirements.

**Q4: What documentation do you provide for regulatory submissions?**
A: We provide design history files, risk management documentation, biocompatibility data, shipping validation reports, and stability data packages tailored to CE-IVD, FDA 510(k), or WHO prequalification requirements.

**Q5: How do you prevent test strip degradation from humidity?**
A: We use aluminum foil pouches with desiccant and oxygen absorber, heat-sealed under low-humidity conditions. WVTR is validated to <0.1 g/m²/day for extended shelf life.

**Q6: Can specimen packaging be customized for specific collection devices?**
A: Absolutely. We design custom tube holders, swab slots, and primary container fits for your specific collection system — ensuring secure transport and intuitive use.
