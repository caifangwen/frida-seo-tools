Challenges

Industry Pain Points

Makeup and fragrance brands live and die by sensory impact. Your packaging must solve complex preservation challenges while delivering the emotional punch that converts a browser into a buyer. Every smudge, every evaporated top note, every broken compact is a customer lost. Here are the critical pain points we address for cosmetics and fragrance houses worldwide.

Fragrance Evaporation

Alcohol-based perfumes lose top notes when exposed to air; packaging must seal scent integrity from factory to first spray

Color Contamination

Eyeshadows, blushes, and lipsticks can smudge or powder-leak; packaging must contain and protect every pigment

Mirror & Mechanism Fragility

Compacts with mirrors, hinges, and click-mechanisms need shock-proof transit without adding bulk

Trend Velocity

Makeup collections change seasonally; packaging must adapt without massive tooling investment

Tester-to-Purchase Gap

In-store testers create desire; takeaway packaging must deliver that same excitement and anticipation

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions we receive from makeup and fragrance brands evaluating packaging partners. If your question is not covered here, our atelier team is available for direct consultation.

How do you prevent fragrance from evaporating or changing in packaging?

We use low-VOC adhesives, sealed constructions, and aroma-barrier films. For ultra-premium fragrances, we include silica gel sachets and recommend humidity-controlled storage. Headspace analysis confirms scent integrity over 12 months.

Can you protect powder products from breakage during shipping?

Yes. Our padded pouches and foam inserts are tested to withstand standard transit drops. For extreme fragility (pressed glitters, baked formulas), we offer suspended-mesh inserts that isolate product from impact.

What is the best packaging for fragrance discovery sets?

Rigid sleeve boxes with individual vial slots prevent breakage and create an elegant "library" aesthetic. We can integrate numbered slots, scent descriptions, and QR-linked fragrance profiles.

How quickly can seasonal makeup collections be packaged?

Digital print and pre-tooled structures enable seasonal packaging in 7-10 days. We recommend developing a "core box + seasonal wrap" system for maximum speed and minimum cost.

Can packaging include actual product swatches or windows?

Absolutely. Clear PET windows with anti-scratch film let customers see actual colors. For security, we can use one-way mirror film or reveal windows that only show when opened.

Is there a sustainable option for fragrance packaging that still feels luxurious?

Yes. Our aluminum coffret cases are infinitely recyclable and feel substantial. Combined with organic cotton linings and plantable outer wraps, they deliver luxury with genuine sustainability credentials.
