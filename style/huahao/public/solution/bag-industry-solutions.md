### ❓ FAQ

**Q1: What is the difference between anti-static, conductive, and shielding bags?**
A: Anti-static bags prevent static generation (pink poly), conductive bags dissipate charges quickly (carbon-loaded), and shielding bags create a Faraday cage effect to protect contents from external ESD events.

**Q2: Do you provide ESD test reports with shipments?**
A: Yes, every batch includes surface resistance and static decay test reports from our certified ESD laboratory.

**Q3: How do I choose the right ESD protection level?**

**Q5: What is your ESD control program in production?**
