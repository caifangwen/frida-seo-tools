FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions we receive from personal care brands evaluating packaging partners. If your question is not covered here, our team is available for direct consultation.

Can your bags really hold heavy family-size bottles without tearing?

Yes. Our reinforced kraft bags with PE-coated bottoms are tested to 10kg dynamic loads. For warehouse club bulk packs, woven PP totes handle 15-20kg confidently with reinforced handles and bottom panels.

Will bags hold up in humid bathroom storage?

Our PE-coated and laminated options resist humidity sagging and mold. For bathroom storage applications, we recommend these treatments over standard uncoated kraft to ensure long-term structural integrity.

How do you communicate refill value to customers?

We design packaging with clear equivalency messaging ("1 pouch = 3 bottles"), visual dilution guides, and QR-linked video tutorials. The packaging itself educates the new behavior and builds confidence in the refill model.

What is the best solution for subscription box programs?

Corrugated boxes with custom inserts sized to your product range, printed with monthly themes, and designed for "shelfie" appeal. Digital print enables cost-effective monthly design changes without plate costs.

Can you differentiate SKUs while maintaining brand family cohesion?

Yes. We develop master brand systems with color-coded variants — consistent logo, typography, and structure with distinct accent colors or patterns per product line. This ensures instant recognition at shelf and in the home.

How sustainable can personal care packaging be at volume?

Very. Our 100% recycled kraft retail bags, compostable refill pouches, and reusable tote programs have been deployed at 10M+ unit scale with cost parity to conventional options. Sustainability and scale are not mutually exclusive.
