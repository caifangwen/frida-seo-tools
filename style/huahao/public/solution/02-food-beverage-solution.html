Food & Beverage Industry Pain Points

The food and beverage industry faces some of the most stringent packaging requirements across all retail sectors. From strict food safety compliance to temperature tolerance and oil resistance, F&B operators need packaging solutions that protect product integrity while enhancing brand visibility during delivery and takeout operations. Understanding these unique challenges is essential for developing effective packaging strategies.

Food Safety Compliance

Strict regulations for food-contact materials including FDA and GB standards compliance requirements.

Oil & Water Resistance

Takeout and delivery packaging must prevent leakage and maintain structural integrity with oily or wet foods.

Temperature Tolerance

Hot and cold food applications require different material properties to maintain product quality and safety.

Brand Visibility

Packaging serves as mobile advertising in delivery scenarios, maximizing brand exposure with every order.

Cost Efficiency

High-volume daily operations need economical yet quality solutions that balance cost with performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our food and beverage packaging solutions. Our technical team is available to provide specific guidance on food safety compliance, material selection, and customization requirements for your F&B operation.

Are your food packaging bags FDA approved?

Yes, all our food-contact materials fully comply with FDA 21 CFR regulations. We provide comprehensive test reports and documentation with each order to ensure your complete regulatory compliance and peace of mind.

Can the bags handle hot foods?

Our standard bags safely withstand temperatures up to 80°C, suitable for most hot food applications. For higher temperature requirements (up to 120°C), we offer specialized heat-resistant materials designed specifically for hot food packaging and delivery.

How effective is the oil resistance?

Our PE-coated bags achieve Kit level 6-8 oil resistance, making them suitable for most oily foods including fried items, BBQ, and greasy bakery products. Custom high-performance coatings are available for extreme oil resistance requirements.

Do you provide tamper-evident options?

Yes, we offer comprehensive tamper-evident security solutions including tamper-evident seals, security stickers, and tear-off strips specifically designed for food delivery safety to ensure product integrity and customer confidence.

Can I order mixed sizes in one shipment?

Absolutely. We fully support mixed SKU orders within a single shipment to meet your diverse packaging needs. This flexibility allows F&B operators to order various bag sizes and types optimized for different menu items in one consolidated delivery.
