## 🎯 Industry Pain Points

- **Degassing dilemma**: Freshly roasted coffee releases CO2 for 48-72 hours; packaging must vent gas without letting oxygen in
- **Oxidation clock**: Coffee staling begins within hours of roasting; oxygen is the enemy of flavor complexity
- **Light and moisture damage**: UV light degrades aromatic oils; moisture causes mold and off-flavors
- **Shelf life vs. freshness perception**: Consumers equate "fresh" with recently roasted; packaging must communicate roast date transparently
- **Third wave aesthetics**: Specialty coffee consumers judge roasters by bag design; generic packaging signals commodity coffee

## ❓ FAQ

**Q1: How do one-way valves actually work?**
A: The valve contains a membrane that opens under internal CO2 pressure (from degassing coffee) but seals against external oxygen. This allows fresh-roasted coffee to be packaged immediately while preventing oxidation.

**Q2: How long does coffee stay fresh in valve bags?**
A: Properly stored whole bean coffee maintains peak flavor 2-4 weeks and acceptable quality 8-12 weeks in foil valve bags. Ground coffee degrades faster — consume within 1-2 weeks.

**Q3: Can compostable bags really match foil barrier performance?**
A: Our compostable valve bags achieve 80-90% of foil barrier performance — adequate for 4-6 week freshness windows. For extended shelf life or export, foil remains the gold standard.

**Q4: Should I use zipper or tin tie for reclosure?**
A: Zippers offer better resealability and consumer convenience but add cost. Tin ties are classic, cost-effective, and signal craft/coffee heritage. Consider your brand positioning and price point.

**Q5: How important is the roast date on packaging?**
A: Critical. Third-wave coffee consumers consider roast date the #1 freshness indicator. "Roasted On" generates more trust than "Best Before" and differentiates specialty from commodity.

**Q6: What bag size is best for my roasting operation?**
A: Café retail typically uses 250g-340g. Grocery uses 340g-454g. Subscriptions often use 340g. Wholesale uses 2.27kg-5kg. We help optimize for your equipment and channel mix.
