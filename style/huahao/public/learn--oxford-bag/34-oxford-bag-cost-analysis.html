06
FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a typical margin from FOB to retail?

Typical multipliers are FOB times 2.5–3 for wholesale, and wholesale times 2–2.5 for retail. A $7 FOB bag might wholesale for $18–$21 and retail for $36–$50. Premium brands with strong marketing can achieve higher multiples.

How much can I save by switching from YKK to SBS zippers?

For a bag with 3 zippers, approximately $0.30–$0.60 FOB at 1K MOQ. At 10K MOQ, savings increase to $0.50–$0.90 per bag. Evaluate whether the savings justify potential quality perception differences.

Is it cheaper to manufacture in Vietnam than China?

For simple bags, Vietnam can be 5–15% cheaper due to labor costs and trade agreement advantages. For complex bags requiring extensive hardware and specialized skills, coastal China often remains competitive due to supply chain density.

What is the biggest hidden cost in bag manufacturing?

Rework and quality failures. A bag that fails inspection requires disassembly, repair, or scrapping—often costing 2–3 times the original labor. Investing in upfront quality control prevents far greater downstream costs.
