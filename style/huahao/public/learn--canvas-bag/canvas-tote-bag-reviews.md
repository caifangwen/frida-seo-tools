## Buying Guide

### What to Look For

1. **Fabric Weight (GSM):** 250+ GSM for daily use, 350+ GSM for heavy loads
2. **Handle Length:** 20"+ for shoulder carry, 12–16" for hand carry
3. **Stitching:** Look for double-stitched or X-stitched stress points
4. **Closure:** Open top is fine for casual use; zipper is essential for commuting
5. **Certifications:** GOTS, OEKO-TEX for eco-friendly assurance

### What to Avoid

- Bags under 200 GSM (too thin for practical use)
- Single-stitched handles (will tear under weight)
- Vague material claims ("premium canvas" without GSM spec)
- Non-removable plastic packaging on "eco-friendly" bags

## Care & Maintenance

A quality canvas tote bag should last **3–5 years** with proper care. Here are the essentials:

### Quick Care Rules

- **Wash cold** with mild detergent (avoid bleach)
- **Air dry only**—never tumble dry canvas
- **Spot clean** minor stains instead of full wash cycles
- **Iron on medium** if needed (canvas wrinkles)

## FAQ

### What is the best GSM for a canvas tote bag?

For everyday use, **280–340 GSM** hits the sweet spot of durability and comfort. For heavy loads (groceries, books, tools), go with **350–450 GSM**. For light promotional giveaways, 200–250 GSM is acceptable.

### Are canvas tote bags machine washable?

Most cotton canvas bags are machine washable on a **gentle, cold-water cycle**. However, bags with leather handles, waxed coatings, or structured bottoms should be hand-washed or spot-cleaned. Always check the care label first.

### How much weight can a canvas tote bag hold?

A well-constructed canvas tote (280+ GSM, reinforced handles) can comfortably hold **15–30 lbs**. Heavy-duty utility totes (400+ GSM) can handle **40–60 lbs**.

### Is canvas better than cotton for tote bags?

Canvas *is* cotton—just woven more tightly and often with a heavier weight. "Canvas vs cotton" is really a question of **canvas weight vs lighter cotton fabric**. For tote bags, canvas is always the better choice due to its durability.

### Can I customize a canvas tote bag with my logo?
