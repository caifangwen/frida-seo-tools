A beautifully designed logo loses impact if poorly positioned. Here's how to maximize brand visibility.

A beautifully designed logo loses impact if it's poorly positioned, too small, or competing with cluttered visuals. Logo placement and print area optimization are where brand strategy meets manufacturing reality.

01
Positions

Standard Print Positions

🅰️

Front Center

Highest visibility. 60–80% of panel width. Primary logo position.

🅱️

Back Panel

Secondary message, website, social handles. Mirror of front for walking exposure.

📐

Side Gussets

Taglines, hashtags, QR codes. Moderate visibility from angles.

📦

Bottom Gusset

Subtle branding, copyright, care instructions. Seen when bag is set down.

05
Mistakes

Common Design Mistakes

❌ Avoid

  • No bleed allowance (white edges show)
  • Hairline borders (< 0.25 pt)
  • Light logo on light bag
  • Overcrowding 5+ elements
  • Ignoring handle intrusion zone

✅ Do

  • 3 mm bleed on all edges
  • Minimum 0.5 pt line weight
  • 30%+ lightness contrast
  • Maximum 3 elements per panel
  • 5–8 cm clear zone at top center
