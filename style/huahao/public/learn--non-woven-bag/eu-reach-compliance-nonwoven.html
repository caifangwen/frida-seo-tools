Exporting nonwoven bags to the European Union requires compliance with REACH—the Registration, Evaluation, Authorization, and Restriction of Chemicals regulation. Non-compliance can result in market denial, product recalls, and significant financial penalties.

01
Framework

REACH SVHC Overview

Substances of Very High Concern (SVHC) are chemicals identified by ECHA as having serious consequences for human health or the environment. The 0.1% threshold applies to any homogeneous component.
