Clothing protection demands a delicate balance: fabrics must shield garments from dust, light, and insects while allowing air circulation that prevents moisture buildup and mildew.

01
Science

Breathability Benefits

Nonwoven PP

  • Air permeability: 200–600 mm/s
  • Vapor transmission: 1,000–3,000 g/m²/24h
  • Condensation risk: Low
  • Temperature buffering: Moderate

Plastic (PE/PVC)

  • Air permeability: Near zero
  • Vapor transmission: 0–50 g/m²/24h
  • Condensation risk: High
  • Temperature buffering: Poor

The mildew mechanism: Plastic bags trap moisture against fabric fibers. Within 48–72 hours in warm conditions, mold activates. Nonwoven's porous structure allows moisture to escape, maintaining RH below 70%.
