Handles are the most stressed component of any bag—and the most noticed by users. A handle that digs into palms, tears under load, or looks flimsy can ruin an otherwise excellent bag.

01
Type A

Heat-Pressed Handles

Separate handle straps are bonded to the bag body using ultrasonic vibration or thermal welding.

⚡

Fast

0.5–1.5 seconds per attachment

💧

Water-Resistant

Welded joint blocks moisture

✨

Clean Look

No visible stitching

Limitation: Welded joints achieve 60–75% of fabric tensile strength. Peel vulnerability under side loads. Cannot be repaired if bond fails.

Comfort Factors

Width

2.5–4.0 cm distributes pressure. Doubling width halves hand pressure.

Length

32–35 cm drop suits most adults for hand or shoulder carry.
