Quality rule: Never go below 7 SPI for load-bearing seams. Low SPI creates long stitch lengths that propagate tears easily.

03
Seams

Seam Types

🔗

Overlock (504)

Edge finishing. Moderate strength. Not for primary structural seams.

⛓️

Chain Stitch (401)

Fast, stretchable. 20–30% faster than lock stitch. Easy to unravel if broken.

🔒

Lock Stitch (301)

Maximum security. Needle and bobbin interlock. Does not unravel.

Quality

Quality Checkpoints

  • Thread tension: Upper 150–200 g; Lower 100–150 g
  • Needle condition checked every 500 bags
  • Stitch length calibrated every 1,000 bags
  • No skip stitches (> 1 skip per 10 cm = reject)
  • No thread breakage or uneven tension
  • Thread ends trimmed to < 3 mm
