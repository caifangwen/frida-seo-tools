Standard nonwoven bags deliver excellent value for basic branding and everyday use. But when your design demands photographic print quality, waterproof protection, or a premium glossy finish, laminated nonwoven bags become the clear choice.

01
Process

What Is Lamination?

Lamination in the nonwoven bag industry typically refers to bonding a thin Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) film to the surface of nonwoven fabric.

01

BOPP Film

PP extruded into film, stretched biaxially, and cooled to 12–30 microns.

02

Printing (Optional)

For pre-printed lamination, BOPP passes through gravure printing press.

03

Adhesive

PE or EVA adhesive is extruded onto the nonwoven fabric.

04

Bonding

Film and nonwoven pass through heated rollers creating permanent bond.
