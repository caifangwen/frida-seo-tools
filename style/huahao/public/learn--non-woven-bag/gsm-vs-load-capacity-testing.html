Manufacturers quote GSM and tensile strength figures, but what do those numbers actually mean when a bag is filled with groceries, books, or bottles? Real-world load testing bridges the gap between laboratory specifications and practical performance.

01
Methodology

Test Methodology

01

Static Load Test

Bags filled with steel shot, hung for 24 hours. Record time to failure.

02

Dynamic Drop Test

Bags filled to 80% capacity, dropped from 1m onto concrete 10 times.

03

Variables

Tested GSM (80, 100), handle type, reinforcement, and seam type.
