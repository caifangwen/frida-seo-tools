EU

EU REACH Compliance

REACH governs all articles placed on the EU market. Key obligations: SVHC threshold of 0.1%, Annex XVII restrictions (lead, cadmium, phthalates, azo dyes), and comprehensive supplier documentation.

04
US

California Proposition 65

Prop 65 requires clear warnings before exposing consumers to 900+ listed chemicals. Key chemicals for nonwoven bags: lead, cadmium, DEHP, DBP. Safe harbor levels determine warning obligations.

→ Deep dive: Prop 65 Guide

ASTM D6400 / EN 13432

Industrial compostability. Required for biodegradable claims.

Greenwashing caution: Vague terms like "eco-friendly" lack legal definition. The FTC Green Guides and EU Green Claims Directive require substantiation for all environmental claims.
