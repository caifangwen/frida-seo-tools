Definition

What Are Non Woven Bags?

Non woven bags are bags made from non woven fabric, which is a sheet or web structure bonded together by entangling fiber or filaments mechanically, chemically, or thermally. Unlike traditional woven fabric made from interlaced yarns, non woven material is produced directly from fibers without the weaving process.

The most common material used is polypropylene (PP), a thermoplastic polymer that creates a lightweight yet durable fabric when processed through spunbond or meltblown manufacturing methods.

Non Woven vs Woven Fabric

1
Traditional Woven Yarn → Weaving → Fabric → Cutting → Sewing → Product

Multiple steps, interlaced yarns, traditional textile process.

2
Non Woven Polymer → Extrusion → Fiber → Web → Bonding → Product

Direct fiber-to-fabric process, faster, lower cost.

The key difference: non woven fabric skips the weaving and yarn production steps, resulting in:

  • Faster production (direct fiber-to-fabric process)
  • Lower cost (fewer manufacturing steps)
  • Consistent quality (no weaving defects)
  • Customizable properties (fiber orientation, density, thickness)

Manufacturing

How Non Woven Bags Are Made

Understanding the manufacturing process helps you evaluate quality and make better sourcing decisions.

01

Raw Material

Polypropylene pellets dried and mixed with additives.

02

Extrusion

Melting at 200-220°C, filtering, metering.

03

Spinning

Spinneret creates filaments, quenching cools them.

04

Web Formation

Filaments laid randomly, web consolidated.

05

Bonding

Thermal calendering or chemical bonding.

06

Finishing

Winding, slitting, QC, packaging.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do non woven bags last?

With normal use, quality non woven bags (100+ GSM) can last 2-5 years with regular use, supporting 50-100+ shopping trips. Lifespan depends on GSM, handle type, and usage patterns.

Can non woven bags be washed?

Yes, non woven bags can be hand washed with mild soap and water. Air dry only—do not machine wash or tumble dry as heat can damage the fabric structure.

What's the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Typical MOQs range from 500-1000 pieces for custom printed bags. Stock bags are available in smaller quantities (100+ pieces). MOQ varies by manufacturer and customization level.

Are non woven bags food safe?

Yes, when manufactured with food-grade PP and appropriate certifications (FDA, LFGB). Always verify food-safe certification for food contact applications.

What's the production lead time?

Standard lead times: Stock bags 3-7 days, custom printed (500-1000 pcs) 10-15 days, large custom orders (5000+ pcs) 20-30 days.

Are non woven bags waterproof?

Non woven PP fabric has natural water resistance but is not fully waterproof. For enhanced water protection, laminated non woven bags with PP film coating are available.

Key Takeaway

Non woven bags represent the sweet spot between cost, durability, and environmental responsibility for modern businesses. As global regulations tighten on single-use plastics and consumers demand sustainable packaging, non woven bags offer a practical solution that doesn't compromise on quality or branding potential.
