Material Science

What Are PP Woven Bags?

PP woven bags (polypropylene woven bags) are industrial packaging solutions made from woven polypropylene tape yarn. They offer exceptional strength-to-weight ratio, moisture resistance, and cost-effectiveness — making them the preferred choice for agricultural, construction, food, and chemical packaging worldwide.

Quick Fact: PP woven bags can carry loads from 5kg to 50kg depending on GSM (grams per square meter) and construction. The material is 100% recyclable (#5 plastic) and requires only ~11 reuses to offset its carbon footprint versus single-use plastic bags.

Key Characteristics of PP Woven Fabric

  • High tensile strength: Woven structure distributes load evenly across all directions
  • Moisture resistant: Polypropylene does not absorb water, protecting contents from humidity
  • Breathable: Micro-gaps between tapes allow air circulation, ideal for agricultural products
  • Chemically inert: No reaction with most chemicals, safe for food and industrial use
  • UV stabilizable: Additives can extend outdoor life from months to 2+ years
  • Printable: Accepts flexographic, rotogravure, and BOPP lamination for branding

Product Types

Types of PP Woven Bags

PP woven bags come in multiple constructions, each optimized for specific applications. Understanding these types helps you select the right packaging for your product.

Laminated Bags

BOPP or PE film coating for moisture barrier and premium printing. Best for retail and food products.

Valve Bags

Self-closing valve for automatic filling. Ideal for cement, flour, and powdered products.

Gusseted Bags

Side folds expand for 3D shape. Perfect for rice, grains, and products needing stable stacking.

UV Stabilized Bags

Additives protect from sun damage. Essential for outdoor storage and construction materials.

Perforated Bags

Micro-holes for ventilation. Critical for fresh vegetables and products requiring air flow.

FIBC Bulk Bags

Ton bags for 500-2000kg loads. Heavy-duty solution for industrial bulk transport.

Technical Specs

Understanding GSM & Load Capacity

GSM (Grams per Square Meter) is the primary metric for woven bag thickness and strength. Higher GSM means heavier fabric, more material, and greater load capacity — but also higher cost.

GSM Range Thickness Typical Load Common Applications Cost Tier
60-70 GSM Light-duty 5-10 kg Light grains, promotional bags Economy
75-85 GSM Standard 10-20 kg Rice, flour, animal feed, retail Standard
90-100 GSM Heavy-duty 20-30 kg Cement, sand, chemicals, export Premium
110-130 GSM Extra heavy 30-50 kg Construction materials, minerals Industrial

Pro tip: 80-100 GSM covers 90% of commercial applications. Only specify heavier fabric if your product has sharp edges, exceeds 25kg, or requires extended outdoor storage.

Recycling Pathways

  • Mechanical recycling: Shredded and re-extruded into new PP products
  • Downcycling: Converted into lower-grade plastic items (park benches, plastic lumber)
  • Energy recovery: Incineration with energy capture (last resort)

Important: PP woven bags are NOT biodegradable. Environmental benefits only materialize through genuine reuse (minimum 11 times) and proper end-of-life recycling. Avoid greenwashing claims.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for PP woven bags?

Standard MOQ is 500 pieces for plain bags, 1000-2000 pieces for custom printed bags depending on printing method. Flexographic printing: 500 pcs minimum. Rotogravure: 2000+ pcs. BOPP lamination: 3000+ pcs. Sample orders (50-100 pcs) available for quality testing.

How long do PP woven bags last?

Indoor storage: 3-5 years without degradation. Outdoor storage (UV-stabilized): 1-2 years depending on sun exposure. Non-UV-stabilized bags left in direct sunlight may degrade within 3-6 months. Actual lifespan depends on GSM, usage frequency, and environmental conditions.

Are PP woven bags waterproof?

Standard woven PP is water-resistant but not waterproof — the woven structure has micro-gaps. For waterproof protection, specify laminated bags (BOPP or PE coating) or add PE/PP liner bags. Laminated bags provide complete moisture barrier for humid environments and outdoor storage.

Can PP woven bags be customized with my logo?

Yes, customization is standard. Provide artwork in AI, PDF, or high-resolution PNG (300 DPI minimum). We offer free digital proofing before production. Color matching to Pantone Solid Coated available. For complex designs, rotogravure or BOPP lamination recommended over flexographic printing.

What is the lead time for bulk orders?

Standard lead time: 15-25 days after sample approval and deposit receipt. Rush orders (10-12 days) available with 15% surcharge. Peak season (August-November) may extend to 30 days. Sample production: 5-7 days. Shipping time additional (3-7 days express, 15-30 days sea freight).
