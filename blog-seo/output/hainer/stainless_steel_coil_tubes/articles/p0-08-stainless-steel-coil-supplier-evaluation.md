## Introduction

Evaluating a **stainless steel coil supplier** requires more than just comparing prices. A comprehensive assessment ensures you partner with a supplier who can deliver consistent quality, reliable service, and long-term value.

This checklist provides a systematic framework for supplier evaluation, helping you:
- **Qualify potential suppliers** efficiently
- **Compare suppliers** objectively
- **Identify risks** before they become problems
- **Build strong supplier relationships** for mutual success

Use this checklist for new supplier qualification or periodic supplier performance reviews.

### How to Use This Checklist

1. **Pre-Qualification**: Initial screening of potential suppliers
2. **On-Site Audit**: Detailed evaluation during factory visits
3. **Scorecard**: Quantitative comparison of multiple suppliers
4. **Periodic Review**: Annual supplier performance assessment

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: How often should suppliers be re-evaluated?

**A**: 
- **Preferred suppliers**: Annual review
- **Approved suppliers**: Annual review
- **Conditional suppliers**: Quarterly review until upgraded
- **After quality incidents**: Immediate re-audit

### Q2: What is the minimum score for supplier approval?

**A**: 
- **Preferred**: ≥9.0
- **Approved**: ≥7.0
- **Conditional**: ≥5.0
- **Not Approved**: <5.0

### Q3: Should I audit traders differently from manufacturers?

**A**: Yes. For traders:
- Focus on their supplier qualification process
- Verify their quality control procedures
- Check their inventory and handling
- Audit their source mills if possible

### Q4: What if a supplier refuses third-party inspection?

**A**: This is a red flag. Consider:
- Understanding their reasons
- Offering to share inspection costs
- Finding alternative suppliers if they persist

### Q5: How many suppliers should I qualify?

**A**: Best practice:
- **2-3 approved suppliers** per material type
- **1 preferred supplier** (primary)
- **1-2 backup suppliers** (secondary)

This balances leverage with supply security.
